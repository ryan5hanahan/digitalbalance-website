Privacy Policy

We collect nothing.

Effective April 19, 2026  ·  Digital Balance by Ontic, LLC

"We built this app to help you reclaim agency. Harvesting your data would undermine everything it stands for."

Who we are

Digital Balance is made by Ontic, LLC. We make a $1 PWA to help people build a healthier relationship with technology. That's it. We are not an ad business, not a data broker, not a SaaS company collecting behavioral signals.

What we collect

Zero Nothing. No analytics. No tracking pixels. No cookies. No fingerprinting. No telemetry. No crash reports. No usage events. We have no idea how you use this app, and that's exactly how we want it.

Where your data lives

All data you create inside Digital Balance — your assessment answers, lesson progress, daily check-ins, awareness log entries, exercise responses — is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. It lives on your device. It never leaves your device. We cannot access it, read it, or delete it. We don't have a server that receives it.

Here's a complete list of what's stored:

  • Your Profile — how you answered the onboarding assessment
  • Lesson Progress — which lessons you've completed, quiz answers, scores
  • Awareness Log — urge level entries and notes
  • Daily Check-ins — wellbeing scores, urge responses, gratitude notes
  • Notification Schedule — when reminder notifications are scheduled
  • App Settings — your theme preference

Third-party services

None. There is no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Mixpanel, no Sentry, no Segment, no Amplitude, no Hotjar, and no CDN that logs requests about you.

The only external request the app makes is loading fonts from Google Fonts (used on this website, not in the app itself). Google's own privacy policy governs that interaction. The app itself makes no external requests after the initial install.

Payment

If you purchased Digital Balance through the App Store, Google Play, or another payment processor, your payment was handled entirely by them under their own privacy policy. We receive confirmation that a purchase occurred — we do not receive, store, or process any payment information, card numbers, or billing details.

Exporting and deleting your data

You can export all your data as a JSON file and delete everything from within the app at any time — no account needed, no email required. The export and delete options are in the Privacy section of the app.

If you delete your data, it's gone. We have no copy to delete because we never had it.

Children

Digital Balance is not directed at children under 13. We don't collect data from anyone — including children — so COPPA doesn't apply in the usual sense. But to be clear: this program is designed for adults working through their relationship with technology.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page and the effective date above. Given that we collect nothing, meaningful changes are unlikely. We won't email you about it because we don't have your email.
